Instructions

Welcome to the Penalty Shot Soccer Game! Here's how to play:

  1. Select a number from 1 to 9 to indicate the direction you want to shoot the ball to.
  2. Click the "Start" button to initiate the game.
  3. The goalkeeper will randomly select a direction to save the ball.
  4. If your direction matches the goalkeeper's, it's a "Miss".
  5. If your direction is different from the goalkeeper's, it's a "Goal".
  6. Click the "Restart" button to play the game again.
